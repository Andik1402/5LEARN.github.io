The level of difficulty of an intermediate level language class can vary depending on the particular language being studied and the level of proficiency of the students in the class. In general, however, an intermediate level language class is typically designed for students who have already acquired a basic level of proficiency in the language and are now ready to start learning more advanced grammar and vocabulary.
